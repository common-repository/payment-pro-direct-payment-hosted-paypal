Accept Credit card payments using PayPal directly on your WooCommerce shop, and issue refunds.

== Description ==

Accept credit cards on any WooCommerce shop using PayPal Payments Pro Direct Payment, the fully branded and customizable payment processing service from PayPal. With no redirects or PayPal branding, you can display the payment form directly in your checkout page to make ordering easy. Plus, save time by issuing refunds / voids directly from the back office Order page. One of our most popular modules!

Support for both PayPal Payments Pro Direct Payment as well as PayPal Payments Pro Hosted Solution is included.

Responsive design and coding ensure the payment form fits any screen size.


Accept standard PayPal payments (Only in Hosted mode) in addition to credit and debit cards.


Embed the payment form directly in the checkout page + refund / void orders directly from the orders tab in the backoffice.


Option to receive an email notification of failed transactions (to help detect fraud).


Choose between Authorization and Capture, or Authorization only and Capture later (when you are ready to ship).


You can later capture any amount directly from the configuration page.


You can refund full / partial orders directly from the configuration page.


Full support for 3D secure.

Full support for PayPal Pro Direct Payment and PayPal Pro Hosted Solution


You can choose the type of credit card you accept and their name & logo will automatically appear in the payment selection page.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How to configure the module
After the module has been installed, navigate to the WooCommerce->Payments page.
Select PayPal Pro and enter your PayPal account credentials.
Choose the account type: Direct Payment or Hosted (Direct payment is currently available only in the US, UK & Canada)
Enter Centinal info (when using 3D Secure).
